### Speakers / sessions

#### 1. Huỳnh Thái Linh — Cloud Quest & Floci

*Level Up Your AWS Skills with Cloud Quest and Floci* — learning AWS via Cloud Quest, billing awareness, and local simulation tools (Floci vs LocalStack).

A talk on the “hidden iceberg” of a project: adopting a **DevOps mindset before disaster** — surfacing delivery, operations, and collaboration risks early instead of reacting only after failure.

### Takeaways & application

- **AWS learning path:** Cloud Quest + billing awareness when practicing on a real account (relevant to **WebFood** deploy).
- **Hackathon & AI project:** packaging a clear problem–solution (SynthHunter) and teamwork under time pressure.
- **Soft skills:** confidence and anti-procrastination habits for long internship projects.
- **Product / DevOps thinking:** surface the project’s “hidden iceberg” early (DevOps before disaster), instead of waiting until failure.

### Experience

Community Day mixed AWS learning, hackathon/AI demos, community products, and soft skills. The AWS Bitexco venue plus First Cloud Journey branding made the community atmosphere tangible.

> Overall, Event 1 broadened my view beyond the serverless lab — from AWS learning and AI projects to product thinking and soft skills.
